Technical Specifications

Supported Calculations

  • Voltage Conversion: AC RMS to DC voltage using VDC = VAC / √2
  • Current Conversion: AC to DC current using IDC = IAC × 0.636
  • Power Calculations: Both AC power (with power factor) and DC power
  • Efficiency Analysis: Theoretical conversion efficiency calculations
  • Power Factor Support: Handles power factor values from 0.1 to 1.0

Input Ranges

  • Voltage: 0.1V to 100,000V (0.1V to 100kV)
  • Current: 0.001A to 10,000A (1mA to 10kA)
  • Power Factor: 0.1 to 1.0 (10% to 100%)
  • Precision: Results calculated to 6 decimal places, displayed to 2 decimal places
